The Mathematics of Search O(S^k) vs O(kS)

In a task of depth k with S options per step:

  • Forward Chaining: Must solve $P = 1/S^k$. Probability of success drops exponentially.
  • Backward Chaining: Solves $k$ individual steps of $P = 1/S$. Probability sum is linear.
